Product Details

A green apple is a tart and crisp fruit, easily recognized by its bright green skin and firm texture. Known for its refreshing, tangy flavor, it is a popular choice for those who enjoy a less sweet apple variety. Green apples are rich in fiber, Vitamin C, and antioxidants, making them a healthy snack. They are also widely used in baking, salads, and juices due to their unique taste and ability to hold shape when cooked.
