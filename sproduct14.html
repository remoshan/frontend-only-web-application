Product Details

An avocado is a creamy, nutrient-dense fruit known for its smooth, greenish-brown skin and rich, buttery texture. Inside, it contains a large seed surrounded by soft, pale green flesh that has a mild, slightly nutty flavor. Avocados are packed with healthy fats, particularly monounsaturated fats, and are rich in vitamins E, K, and C, as well as potassium and fiber. They are commonly used in guacamole, salads, sandwiches, or enjoyed on their own with seasonings. Due to their health benefits, avocados are considered a superfood.
